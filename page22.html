Seniors Trophies

Alex Thomson Trophy
Presented by Alex Thomson in 1993
Pairs Match play knock out competition. Four ball better ball, Handicap 90% difference off the lowest in the four ball. Played from the yellow tees May to September.

Ann Hughes Trophy
Presented by Barry Hughes in 1999 in memory of his wife
Drawn pairs Ladies and Seniors Stroke play competition, best nett score.

Brian Humphrey Cup and Wooden Spoon
Presented by Brian Humphrey (Seniors Captain 2001)
Four ball better ball match play competition between Captain’s and Vice Captain’s teams. Handicap 90% difference off the lowest in the four ball. Played in September. Winner receives the Cup, losers the wooden spoon

Captain’s Challenge Cup
Presented by Wolfgang Kastner in 2005. In appreciation of the Seniors Captains
Awarded to the winner of the Captains Awayday.

Clarendon Trophy
Presented by Chris Shirley in 1998
Awarded to the winner of the first round of the Dewplan Trophy
A stapleford competition played off full handicap from white tees, played in September. This trophy cannot be won by the winner of the Dewplan Trophy.

Seniors Club Championship Shield
Presented to the Seniors by WHGC in 1992
Stroke play competition of two rounds played off full handicap from white tees. Played in June
Awarded to the Senior with the best Gross score for the two rounds.

Dewplan Trophy
Presented by Chris Shirley in 1992
The aggregate of two rounds of stapleford played off full handicap from yellow tees. Played in September.
