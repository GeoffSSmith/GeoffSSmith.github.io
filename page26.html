My Year As Seniors Captain Articles - Introduction by Colin Quelch

As part of the development of our Seniors Website, I consider it is important for us to have an understanding of the history of the Seniors Section, and to this end, when requesting entries for the competitions last year, I provided you with some background on each. I have also included the Roll of Honour on the website as a permanent reference point. In addition, I will be asking our past Captains to provide a record of their year as Captain. 

I recently met with Barry Townsend.  Barry was Captain in 1999. Barry has a long association with the club, and during our discussion Barry was very complimentary regarding the work undertaken by many hardworking members, who have given their time for the benefit of the members of the Seniors. He did not once mention himself, although Barry has over the years contributed as much, if not more than most. Thank you Barry. 
During our chat, Barry was keen to find out about the website and handicap master system, Barry was very interested in the future, advising that he considered that the opportunity to keep members informed and up to date through communication is the key to our success.
In closing Barry provided me with a look back at the past with copies of the Wycombe Heights Golf Club annual newsletter. Click on the 'From the Archive' article, I hope you find them interesting, with some familiar names being featured.

Colin Quelch
