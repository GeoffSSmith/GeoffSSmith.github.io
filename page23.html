Wednesday Roll Up

WHGC Seniors run Wednesday roll ups throughout the year

Early tee times: 
April - October: 7.44 - 8.40, register by 8.00
November - March: 8.16 - 9.12, register by 8.30 

Late tee times: 
10.00 - 10.24

A number of roll up rounds are designated as handicap qualifying rounds and some of these contribute to the PH Cup.
